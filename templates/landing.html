Your virtual social playground.

Welcome to Polaris, a simple social media platform designed for people to connect with everyone and enjoy instead of private chats.

Features:

A custom points system called polar points inspired by the karma system of Reddit. Go ahead and show everyone who has the best posts!

Profile customization including changeable pfps, banners and user description. Personalize your account and make it uniquely yours!

A custom reward system that can grant you exclusive badges so that you can flex all your achievements to your friends.
